"I'm just a simple man trying to make his way through the galaxy."-Boba Fett

Hello there! I'm Zak and as you may have guessed, this is my webpage! I'm currently in my 2nd year at Alfred State and I am 19 years old. I'm going to school for Digital Media and Animation. I hope to one day work for Marvel Studios and help bring to life all my favorite characters and stories. To piggyback off of this, I love quite a few of nerdy things; and with no shame! I love Marvel, DC, Star Wars, Pixar, you name it! When I'm not rewatching a Marvel movie for the 50th time, you can find me either reading, gaming, or doing school work.

Currently, I'm reading through The Two Towers by J.R.R. Tolkien. After finishing this, I plan to move on to the third and final part of The Lord of the Rings trilogy. Aside from the occasional novel, I mostly read graphic novels; Im currently reading my way through Eric Kripke's The Boys series. Graphic Novels are some of my favorite media because on top of getting a kick-ass story, you also get kick-ass art to go with it. Over quarantine, I read a vast amount of graphic novels including Joshua Williams' run of The Flash and Tim Sale's The Long Halloween. As of 2021, the most recent graphic novel I read was The Vision by Tom King!

If you were to observe an ordinary day in my life, you would find that about 50% of my days are spent gaming; whether it be on my PS4, Xbox One, or Nintendo Switch. At most times, I'm usually playing more than one game at a time. I just recently finished both Resident Evil 3 and 7 and Super Mario Galaxy. Currently, I'm playing my way through Super Mario 3D World and Red Dead Redemption 2 for the second time; Red Dead 2 is one of those games that really resonated with me similiar to The Last of Us: Part 2. Gaming is one of my hobbies that I can do almost whenever and still be entertained with it. I view videogames as playable movies in some ways; with my extreme love of cinema, I'm sure you can see the appeal to videogames.
